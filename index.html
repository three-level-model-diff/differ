Differ Visualize changes in documents

With this page you can see how the library works. There are different styles of visualization.

Word Style

The changes are shown in the document: red ones are cancellations of text, green ones are insertions of text and the ones with borders are structural changes

Wiki Style

The changes are shown in a comparative way: in the card on the left is shown the old version and in the card on the right the new one

Git Style

The changes are shown in a comparative way: in the card on the left is shown the old version and in the card on the right the new one


Choose a document to see them in action!
